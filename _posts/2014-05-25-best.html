Information

For many fields, there are "canonical" places on the Internet, where enthusiasts (and specialists) in this field gather, and where one should go for information.

Often, there are "canonical" places to buy stuff. Sometimes, there is a product that is clearly best in its category. Here is my list of such things :)

Where to buy

Books (and everything else ;)): Amazon
Shoes: Zappos
Tires: TireRack

Computers

Buy computer components: Newegg.

Buy optical disc blanks: Supermedia.
Real keyboards information: GeekHack, Reddit, keyboardlover.
Buy real keyboards: Elitekeyboards, wasdkeyboards, Unicomp.
Broadband Internet connections information: Broadband Reports.
